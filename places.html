Places of interest nearby

The Sandstone Point Hotel

The Sandstone Point Hotel is a 10-15 minute drive offering a number of different menu/dining options. Ideal place for a friendly game of football to listen to live music. Concert nights featuring The Beach Boys/ Jimmy Barnes, to name a few, have proved very popular with tickets selling out fast. Transport is provided to and from the complex if required.

Find out more at http://sandstonepointhotel.com.au.

Steve Irwin's Australia Zoo

Steve Irwin's Australia Zoo is approximately a 45 minute drive and a great place to spend the day

Find out more at http://www.australiazoo.com.au.

Aussie World Theme Park

Aussie World Theme Park is approximately a 45 minute drive, however there are the well known theme parks on the Gold Coast if preferred.

Find out more at http://www.aussieworld.com.au and http://www.experienceoz.com.au/Gold_Coast.

Emanudi Markets

Emanudi Markets are open every Wednesday and Sunday.

Find out more at www.eumundimarkets.com.au.

Water Park/Fishing Park

This is a great place to visit to practise your fishing and take in the water activities at the water park.

Find out more at www.blibliwatersports.com.au.

Sunshine Coast

A great day out is to take in the Sunshine Coast.

Find out more at http://www.visitsunshinecoast.com.
