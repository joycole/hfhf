Location

43 Blueberry Street, Banksia Beach, 4507, Queensland

We are located on the 8th Tee at Pacific Harbour Golf Country Club. The area is lush with native foila and at nights we often have kangaroo's saying "gidday" through our boundary fence. Sunsets over the surrounding hills are a treat all locals enjoy.

Brisbane International Airport and the Sunshine Coast are approximately an hours drive. We are happy to collect guests from Brisbane Airport for a small charge. Bribie Island has many beaches which are within a 5-15min drive from our home.
