Our Home

Our new home is luxurious with all modern amenities, we are sure you will enjoy your stay immensely. We have two queen bedrooms (one with ensuite) available plus a twin bedroom with king singles. A lounge area with TV, tea/coffee making facilities and refrigerator is available specifically for the use of our guests. Guests are also welcome to use the barbeque/microwave located in the alfresco area. A selection of home baking is provided at all times.

Outside dining and relaxing is available throughout the days of your stay in the alfresco area, which leads to our 9.5mt swimming pool. We have golf putting/chipping greens for guest use. Landscaped gardens with stunning views down the golf course and lake are ideal for relaxing on our sun lounges enjoying the wonderful climate.

We have a media room with Foxtel sport available, great for catching your favourite game. Guests are free to use all in and outside areas of our home except for our private area.

Guests are invited to arrive early to enjoy our home.
