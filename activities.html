Activities

Golf

Golf packages, including cart, are available.

Tennis Courts

Available to our guests by the Pacific Harbour Golf Club.

Gym/Spa/Sauna/Steam Room

Available to our guests for a small charge by the Pacific Harbour Golf Club.

Beaches

We have many beautiful beaches within a five to fifteen minute drive from our home.

Find out about beach hopping on Bribie Island here.

Dolphins

Dolphins are a common site along the Pumicestone Passage beaches.

Watersports

Kayaks, paddle boards, motorised bikes, plus many other activities are available for hire at Pumicestone Passage.

Find out more at www.bribiehirehut.com.au.

Diving/Snorkelling

Dive Hive is a great outlet to arrange a scuba dive or snorkelling trip. You may be lucky enough to see turtles or watch octopus fight!

Find out more at www.divehive.com.au .
