Breakfast (provided)

We have immense pride in serving our guests with breakfasts catering for all dietary requirements. Breakfasts consist of a wide range of cereals, selection of cold meats/nuts/dried fruits, fresh tropical fruit, yoghurts, bread/croissants/toast, with a wide range of condiments. Fruit juices, fresh fruit smoothies, cafetiere coffee/tea/herbal teas are available. Breakfast can be enjoyed either outside in the fantastic Queensland climate or in our spacious living/dining area and is available from 7.00am.

Lunch

Lunch is available daily at the Pacific Harbour Golf Club Cafe, which is a short stroll from our home. There are a number of cafes/restaurants/clubs on the Island offering appetising lunch specials.

Dinner

Restaurants on the island feature a wide range of cuisine. A free shuttle service is provided to local clubs. Guests are welcome to enjoy our alfresco area with barbeque or have local takeaways overlooking the golf course and swimming pool.
